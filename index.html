Everything You Need to Run Your Organization

HopeOS is packed with powerful features designed to simplify your operations from day one.

Flexible Assessment Programs

Set dues to be yearly, quarterly, or monthly. Bill a uniform amount to all members or customize it for each one.

Automated Notifications

Automatic invoices and payment reminders are sent via email, saving you hours of follow-up work.

Multiple Payment Options

Accept Credit/Debit Cards and ACH bank transfers online. Plus, easily track manual payments like checks, Zelle, or Venmo.

Hierarchical Membership

Manage individuals directly or as members of a parent congregation, reflecting your organization's true structure.

Smart Reconciliation

Securely link your bank account via Plaid to easily match incoming manual payments to outstanding assessments.

Bank-Grade Security

With mandatory verification via Stripe (KYB/KYC) and support for MFA, your data and finances are secure.
